About The Company

Kay's Delights was Founded by a self-taught home baker with a big dream and an even bigger sweet tooth, Kay’s Delights is all about bringing joy through simple, delicious treats. What started as a hobby in my kitchen has grown into a small business fueled by creativity and a deep love for baking. At Kay’s Delights, we specialize in handcrafted biscuits that are made from scratch using quality ingredients and time-tested techniques. Whether you’re after classic flavors or something a little more adventurous, every bite is made to delight. Thank you for supporting a homegrown dream. I can’t wait to share my biscuits with you!

The Heart Behind the Oven

Meet the Owner

Born and raised in the close-knit community of Elim, I’ve always found joy in creating and sharing food. As a proud mother of two amazing sons — one of whom is now my assistant — family remains at the heart of everything I do. What started as a simple hobby soon became a passion, as I discovered how therapeutic baking could be. I love experimenting with new recipes, blending tradition with creativity, and bringing comfort and sweetness into people’s lives, one biscuit at a time.

My Right Hand – My Son

Having my son by my side as my assistant has been one of the most rewarding parts of this journey. His energy, creativity, and dedication bring a fresh spark to our baking days, and working together has strengthened not only our business but our bond as well. Whether it’s helping in the kitchen, managing orders, or adding his own twist to a recipe, he plays an important role in everything we do — and I couldn’t be prouder.
